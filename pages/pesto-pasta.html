Pesto Pasta

Home

Pesto pasta is easy to make and full of flavor. It tastes good hot or cold!

Prep Time: 5 mins

Cook Time: 10 mins

Total Time: 15 mins

Servings: 8

Pesto Pasta Ingredients

  • 1 lb pasta
  • 2 cups fresh basil leaves
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/3 cup pine nuts
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a food processor, combine basil, parmesan cheese, olive oil, pine nuts, and garlic. Process until smooth.
  3. In a large bowl, combine pasta and pesto. Toss until pasta is well coated.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve and enjoy!
